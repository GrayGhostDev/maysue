Medicaid Coverage Information

LovedOneHomeCare is an approved Medicaid provider for eligible home health services. We work with state Medicaid programs to ensure qualifying individuals receive the care they need.

Medicaid Eligibility

Medicaid eligibility varies by state but generally covers:

  • Low-income individuals and families
  • Pregnant women
  • People with disabilities
  • Elderly individuals who meet income requirements

Our team can help you determine if you qualify for Medicaid coverage in your state.

Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS) Waivers

Many states offer Medicaid HCBS waivers that provide additional services beyond standard Medicaid coverage. These waivers are designed to help individuals remain in their homes rather than requiring institutional care.

HCBS waiver services may include:

  • Personal care services
  • Homemaker services
  • Home health aide services
  • Adult day health services
  • Respite care
  • Case management

Medicaid Application Process

The Medicaid application process involves:

  1. Completing a Medicaid application through your state's Medicaid office or healthcare marketplace
  2. Providing documentation of income, assets, and medical needs
  3. Undergoing an assessment to determine the level of care needed
  4. Working with a case manager to develop a care plan

Our team can provide guidance throughout this process and help you navigate the Medicaid system.
