Transportation Services

Our transportation services provide reliable, safe, and comfortable transportation for patients to and from medical appointments, therapy sessions, and other healthcare-related destinations. We understand that transportation barriers can significantly impact access to healthcare, and our goal is to eliminate these obstacles for our clients.

Our Transportation Services Include:

  • Medical Appointment Transportation: Reliable transportation to doctor appointments, hospital visits, therapy sessions, and other medical facilities.
  • Wheelchair-Accessible Vehicles: Specially equipped vehicles to accommodate patients with mobility challenges and wheelchair requirements.
  • Door-to-Door Service: Assistance from the patient's residence to the healthcare facility and back, ensuring safety throughout the journey.
  • Scheduled and On-Demand Services: Regular scheduled transportation for recurring appointments and on-demand options for urgent needs.
  • Long-Distance Medical Transportation: Services for patients requiring transportation to specialized medical facilities outside their immediate area.
  • Post-Procedure Transportation: Safe transport home after surgeries, procedures, or treatments when patients cannot drive themselves.

Our Approach

At May Sue Case Management, our transportation approach prioritizes safety, reliability, and compassionate service. We understand that each patient has unique needs, and we tailor our transportation services accordingly. Our process includes:

  • Personalized Assessment: Evaluation of each client's transportation needs, including mobility requirements, schedule preferences, and special accommodations.
  • Professional Drivers: Our drivers are trained in patient assistance, safety protocols, and emergency procedures to ensure a secure journey.
  • Well-Maintained Vehicles: Regular maintenance and cleaning of our fleet to provide a safe and comfortable transportation experience.
  • Coordination with Healthcare Providers: Communication with medical facilities to ensure timely arrivals for appointments and appropriate post-appointment care.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Accommodating both advance bookings and urgent transportation needs to support comprehensive healthcare access.
