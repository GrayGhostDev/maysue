Private Pay Options

At LovedOneHomeCare, we understand that not all services may be covered by insurance, Medicare, or Medicaid. We offer flexible private pay options to ensure you receive the care you need.

Transparent Pricing

We believe in complete transparency regarding our pricing:

  • Clear hourly rates with no hidden fees
  • Customized care plans to fit your budget
  • Detailed monthly statements
  • No long-term contracts required

Flexible Payment Schedules

We offer various payment options to accommodate your needs:

  • Weekly billing
  • Bi-weekly billing
  • Monthly billing
  • Multiple payment methods accepted (credit card, check, electronic funds transfer)

Service Packages

We offer service packages that can be more economical than hourly rates:

  • Basic Care Package: Includes essential personal care and light housekeeping
  • Comprehensive Care Package: Includes personal care, meal preparation, medication reminders, and housekeeping
  • Premium Care Package: Includes all services plus transportation, shopping, and enhanced companionship

All packages can be customized to meet your specific needs and budget.

Additional Funding Sources

We can help you explore other potential funding sources:

  • Veterans benefits (Aid & Attendance)
  • Long-term care insurance
  • Life insurance conversion
  • Reverse mortgages
  • Community and non-profit assistance programs

Contact us for a free consultation to discuss your care needs and develop a payment plan that works for you.
