Companion Care Services

Our companion care services focus on providing emotional support, friendship, and social engagement to enhance the quality of life for our clients. Loneliness and isolation can significantly impact health and well-being, and our compassionate companions help clients stay connected and engaged.

Conversation & Companionship

Meaningful conversation, reminiscing, and genuine companionship to combat loneliness.

Recreational Activities

Engaging in games, hobbies, and favorite activities to stimulate the mind and promote joy.

Reading & Mail

Reading books, newspapers, or mail aloud and assistance with letter writing or email.

Accompanied Outings

Accompaniment to social events, appointments, shopping, or simply enjoying outdoor walks.

Appointment Reminders

Assistance with keeping track of appointments and social engagements.

Family Connection

Help with phone calls, video chats, and staying connected with family and friends.

Our companion care services can be scheduled for a few hours a week or daily visits, depending on your needs and preferences. Companions can also provide light housekeeping and meal preparation.
