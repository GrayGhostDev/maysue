Dental Case Management Services

Our dental case management services provide comprehensive coordination of oral healthcare for patients with complex dental needs. We bridge the gap between dental care providers and patients, ensuring that individuals receive appropriate, timely, and coordinated dental services that align with their overall healthcare plan.

Our Dental Case Management Services Include:

  • Dental Care Coordination: Comprehensive management of dental appointments, treatments, and follow-up care across multiple providers.
  • Oral Health Assessment: Evaluation of dental needs and development of personalized oral healthcare plans that integrate with overall health management.
  • Dental Insurance Navigation: Assistance with understanding dental benefits, filing claims, and resolving insurance issues to maximize coverage.
  • Special Needs Dental Coordination: Specialized support for patients with disabilities, cognitive impairments, or medical conditions requiring adapted dental care.
  • Dental-Medical Integration: Coordination between dental providers and medical teams to ensure comprehensive care for patients with conditions affecting both oral and overall health.
  • Emergency Dental Care Management: Rapid response coordination for urgent dental needs, including finding providers and arranging immediate care.

Our Approach

At May Sue Case Management, our dental case management approach is patient-centered and focused on improving oral health outcomes. We recognize the critical connection between oral health and overall well-being, and we work to ensure that dental care is integrated into the complete healthcare picture. Our process includes:

  • Comprehensive Dental Assessment: Thorough evaluation of oral health status, dental history, and specific needs to develop an effective care plan.
  • Provider Network Development: Establishing relationships with quality dental providers who can address various specialized needs, from pediatric to geriatric dentistry.
  • Treatment Plan Coordination: Working with dentists and specialists to create cohesive treatment plans that address immediate concerns and long-term oral health.
  • Patient Education: Providing information and resources on oral hygiene, preventive care, and the importance of dental health in overall wellness.
  • Ongoing Monitoring: Regular follow-up to ensure adherence to dental care plans, appointment attendance, and resolution of any emerging issues.

The Importance of Oral Health

Good oral health is essential for overall well-being. Poor dental health has been linked to various health conditions, including heart disease, diabetes, and respiratory infections. Our dental case management services help ensure that oral healthcare is not overlooked in your comprehensive health plan.
