Our Services

Physical Therapy

Our physical therapy services focus on restoring movement and function, reducing pain, and preventing further injury through personalized treatment plans.

Occupational Therapy

Our occupational therapy services help patients develop, recover, and improve the skills needed for daily living and working after illness or injury.

Case Management

Our case management services coordinate healthcare needs, advocate for patients, and ensure access to appropriate resources for optimal outcomes.

Transportation

Our reliable transportation services ensure patients can access their medical appointments and healthcare services without worry or difficulty.

Dental Case Management

Our specialized dental case management services coordinate comprehensive oral healthcare, ensuring patients receive appropriate dental treatments and follow-up care.

About May Sue Case Management

May Sue Case Management is a dedicated healthcare provider committed to delivering exceptional care and support to our patients. With years of experience in the healthcare industry, we understand the unique challenges faced by individuals requiring physical therapy, occupational therapy, and case management services.

Our team of highly qualified professionals works collaboratively to create personalized treatment plans that address each patient's specific needs and goals. We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare that considers not only physical well-being but also emotional and social factors.

Ongoing support and guidance throughout the recovery process
