Insurance Coverage Information

At LovedOneHomeCare, we work with most major insurance providers to help you maximize your benefits and minimize out-of-pocket expenses.

Accepted Insurance Plans

We accept a wide range of insurance plans, including but not limited to:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Humana
  • Kaiser Permanente
  • Long-term Care Insurance
  • Workers' Compensation
  • Auto Insurance (for accident-related care)

Insurance Verification Process

Our team will assist you with the insurance verification process:

  1. We collect your insurance information during the initial consultation
  2. Our insurance specialists verify your coverage and benefits
  3. We provide you with a clear explanation of covered services and any potential out-of-pocket costs
  4. We handle all insurance billing and paperwork on your behalf

Coverage for Home Health Services

Many insurance plans cover home health services when they are:

  • Medically necessary
  • Ordered by a physician
  • Provided by qualified healthcare professionals
  • Part of a documented plan of care

Coverage varies by plan and policy. Our team will work with you to determine what services are covered under your specific insurance plan.
